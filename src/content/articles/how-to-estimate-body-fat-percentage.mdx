The scale shows total weight. **Body fat percentage** shows how much of that weight is fat vs. lean tissue — often a more useful number than BMI alone if you are building muscle or losing fat.

## Why body fat matters

Two people can share the same BMI but different body composition. Higher body fat — especially around the abdomen — is linked to greater metabolic and cardiovascular risk in many studies.

Tracking body fat over months (not days) helps you see whether weight loss is mostly fat or muscle.

## Method 1: Tape measure (US Navy formula)

Measure with a flexible tape, standing relaxed:

- **Neck** — narrowest point below the larynx
- **Waist** — at navel level, not sucked in
- **Hips** (women only) — widest point

Enter measurements in our [Body Fat Calculator](/tools/body-fat-calculator) for an estimate. Accuracy is typically within **3–4%** of professional methods for most people — good enough for trends.

## Method 2: BMI-based estimate (no tape)

If you do not have a tape measure, a formula using BMI, age, and sex gives a rough estimate. Less accurate, but useful as a starting point — available in the same calculator.

## General body fat reference ranges

| Category | Men | Women |
|----------|-----|-------|
| Essential fat | 2–5% | 10–13% |
| Athletes | 6–13% | 14–20% |
| Fitness | 14–17% | 21–24% |
| Average | 18–24% | 25–31% |

Individual targets vary. Health is not defined by reaching "athlete" levels.

## How to lower body fat safely

1. **Moderate calorie deficit** — use our [Calorie Deficit Calculator](/tools/calorie-deficit-calculator)
2. **Adequate protein** — protects muscle during weight loss ([Protein Calculator](/tools/protein-calculator))
3. **Resistance training** — preserves lean mass
4. **Daily movement** — walking adds up ([Walking Calories](/tools/walking-calorie-calculator))

Aim for **0.5–1% body fat loss per month** as a sustainable pace — not crash diets.

## Professional options

DEXA scans, hydrostatic weighing, and some bioimpedance devices are more accurate. Worth considering if you are training seriously or your doctor recommends closer monitoring.

## When to see a doctor

Very low body fat can disrupt hormones and immunity. Rapid loss without trying, or inability to gain healthy weight, also deserves medical evaluation.
